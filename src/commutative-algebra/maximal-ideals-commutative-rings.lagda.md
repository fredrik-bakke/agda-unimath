## Idea

**Maximal ideals** in a
[commutative ring](commutative-algebra.commutative-rings.md) `A` are proper
ideals `I` such that any
[ideal](commutative-algebra.ideals-commutative-rings.md) `J` such that `I ⊆ J`
satisfies `1 ∉ J ⇒ I ＝ J`.
